<p><strong>The 3D Display System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Volume Display</li><li>Solid-Volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D display system market includes swept-volume and solid-volume displays. Swept-volume displays create the illusion of 3D images by projecting light into a defined space, allowing viewers to perceive depth as the images move. Solid-volume displays, on the other hand, generate tangible 3D images that occupy physical space, enabling interaction and manipulation. Both types cater to various applications, including entertainment, medical imaging, and industrial design, enhancing user experience by providing immersive visual environments.</p></p>
