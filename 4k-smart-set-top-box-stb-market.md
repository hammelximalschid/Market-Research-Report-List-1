<p><p>The 4K Smart Set Top Box (STB) market encompasses various types of technologies. IPTV delivers content over the internet, providing access to a wide range of channels and on-demand services. Satellite STBs use satellite signals to transmits television programming, especially in remote areas. DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television) receives broadcast channels via antennas. OTT (Over-the-Top) streaming bypasses traditional distribution networks, allowing direct access to content over the internet. Hybrid STBs combine multiple types, offering flexibility in content delivery methods for users.</p></p>
