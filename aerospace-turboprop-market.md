<p><p>An aerospace turboprop is a type of aircraft propulsion system that combines a gas turbine engine with a propeller to generate thrust. It is widely used in regional and short-haul aircraft due to its fuel efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and ability to operate on shorter runways.
