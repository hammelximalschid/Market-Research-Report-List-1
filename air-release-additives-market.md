<p><strong>The Air Release Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Particles</li><li>Lotion</li><li>Dispersant</li><li>Oil</li><li>Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air release additives are substances that enhance the performance of coatings, inks, and other formulations by reducing trapped air. The market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Solid Particles** - These are inert materials that aid in air release through their physical presence.</p><p>2. **Lotion** - A fluid formulation that provides lubrication and facilitates air expulsion.</p><p>3. **Dispersant** - Agents that help uniformly distribute components, preventing air entrapment.</p><p>4. **Oil** - Used for its low surface tension properties to help release air bubbles.</p><p>5. **Paste** - A thick formulation that encapsulates air, releasing it during application for a smoother finish.</p></p>
