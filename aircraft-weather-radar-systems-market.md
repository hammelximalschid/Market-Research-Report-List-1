<p><strong>The Aircraft Weather Radar Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Polarization Aircraft Weather Radar Systems</li><li>Dual Polarization Aircraft Weather Radar Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Weather Radar Systems market includes two primary types: Single Polarization and Dual Polarization systems. Single Polarization systems transmit and receive radar signals in one plane, primarily detecting precipitation and its intensity. In contrast, Dual Polarization systems utilize both horizontal and vertical signals, providing enhanced data on raindrop shapes and sizes, improving precipitation classification and overall weather assessment. This advanced capability in Dual Polarization systems allows for better forecasting and situational awareness in aviation operations.</p></p>
