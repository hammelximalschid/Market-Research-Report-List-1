<p><ul><li>Hot Plating</li><li>Electrophoresis</li><li>Vacuum Evaporation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminised steel sheet market is segmented into various types based on coating methods: hot plating, electrophoresis, and vacuum evaporation. Hot plating involves the immersion of steel in molten aluminum, providing excellent corrosion resistance. Electrophoresis uses an electric charge to apply a protective aluminum coating, improving adhesion and durability. Vacuum evaporation involves depositing aluminum in a vacuum chamber, resulting in a thin, uniform layer that enhances aesthetic appeal and functionality. Each method offers distinct advantages for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
