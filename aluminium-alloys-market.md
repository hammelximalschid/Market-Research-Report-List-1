<p><p>The aluminium alloys market is primarily divided into two categories: deformed aluminium alloys and cast aluminium alloys. Deformed aluminium alloys, often used in applications requiring high strength and formability, are processed through methods like rolling and extrusion. In contrast, cast aluminium alloys are created by pouring molten aluminium into molds, allowing for more intricate shapes and designs, making them ideal for automotive and aerospace components. Both categories cater to diverse industries, reflecting the versatility of aluminium as a material.</p></p>
