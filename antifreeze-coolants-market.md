<p><strong>The Antifreeze & Coolants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inorganic Acid Technology</li><li>Organic Acid Technology</li><li>Hybrid Organic Acid Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antifreeze and coolants market is primarily categorized into three types: Inorganic Acid Technology (IAT), Organic Acid Technology (OAT), and Hybrid Organic Acid Technology (HOAT). IAT uses inorganic compounds like silicates and phosphates, offering short-term protection but requiring frequent changes. OAT relies on organic acids, providing long-lasting performance and superior corrosion protection. HOAT combines both, integrating benefits from IAT and OAT, ensuring effective cooling and extended life. Each type caters to different vehicle requirements and maintenance preferences.</p></p>
