<p><p>An articulated bus is a type of bus designed with an extended length and a flexible joint, allowing it to navigate urban areas while accommodating a higher passenger capacity. This design typically features two or more rigid sections connected by a pivoting joint, enabling better maneuverability in tight spaces compared to traditional buses. Articulated buses are widely used in public transportation systems, particularly in densely populated cities, due to their efficiency in reducing traffic congestion and improving passenger flow.</p><p>The Articulated Bus Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period. Electric and hybrid articulated buses are gaining traction as cities aim to reduce carbon emissions and adopt greener alternatives.</p><p>Key trends in the market include the integration of smart technologies, such as real-time tracking and automated systems, to enhance operational efficiency and passenger experience.
