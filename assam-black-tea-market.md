<p><p>Assam Black Tea is primarily categorized into two market types: Orthodox and CTC (Crush, Tear, Curl). Orthodox tea is produced using traditional methods, resulting in whole leaves that offer complex flavors and aromas, appealing to connoisseurs. In contrast, CTC tea is mechanically processed, producing smaller, tea dust-like particles that brew quickly and create a robust, strong flavor, often favored for masala chai and mass-market blends. Both types play crucial roles in Assam's tea economy, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
