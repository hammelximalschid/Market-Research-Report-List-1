<p><p>The automotive brake ECU market is segmented into two main types: Disc Brake ECU and Drum Brake ECU. Disc Brake ECUs are designed for vehicles equipped with disc braking systems, which provide superior stopping power and heat dissipation. In contrast, Drum Brake ECUs cater to vehicles using drum brake systems, which are simpler and often lighter, though typically less efficient than disc brakes. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety, control, and overall braking performance.</p></p>
