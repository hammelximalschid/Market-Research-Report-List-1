<p><p>The automotive fuel injector market is segmented into three primary types: Gasoline Port Injection, Gasoline Direct Injection, and Diesel Direct Injection. Gasoline Port Injection delivers fuel into the intake manifold, improving fuel atomization and combustion efficiency. Gasoline Direct Injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing power and fuel economy while reducing emissions. Diesel Direct Injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber as well, optimizing performance and efficiency for diesel engines. Each type serves distinct engine designs and performance demands.</p></p>
