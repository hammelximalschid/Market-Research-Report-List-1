<p><p>In the Automotive Selective Laser Melting (SLM) metal 3D printing market, machines are categorized into two types: single laser and multiple laser systems. Single laser machines utilize one laser source to melt metal powders layer by layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for smaller production runs. In contrast, multiple laser machines deploy several lasers simultaneously, significantly enhancing productivity and efficiency for larger-scale manufacturing. This distinction allows automotive manufacturers to choose technology based on their production needs, speed, and budget constraints.</p></p>
