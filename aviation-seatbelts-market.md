<p><p>The aviation seatbelts market encompasses various types designed to enhance passenger safety and comfort. Two-point seatbelts consist of a lap belt secured across the hips, suitable for lightweight aircraft. Three-point seatbelts add a shoulder strap, providing better restraint in turbulence and is common in commercial airlines. Four-point seatbelts feature additional straps to secure the shoulders and lap, often used in high-performance or military aviation. Other variations may include specialized designs for differing aircraft configurations and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
