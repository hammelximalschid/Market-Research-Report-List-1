<p><p>The biotechnology separation systems market includes various techniques for isolating and purifying biomolecules. Membrane filtration uses semi-permeable membranes for selective separation. Liquid chromatography separates compounds based on their interaction with a stationary phase. Centrifuges utilize centrifugal force to separate components by density. Electrophoresis relies on the movement of charged particles in an electrical field to achieve separation. Flow cytometry quantifies and sorts cells based on their physical and chemical characteristics. Other methods include extraction and phase separation techniques.</p></p>
