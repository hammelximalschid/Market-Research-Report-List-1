<p><p>Board management software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, providing accessibility from any device with internet access, facilitating collaboration and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on a company's hardware, offering greater control over data and security but may limit accessibility. Each type caters to different organizational needs, balancing convenience and control in board governance and meeting management.</p></p>
