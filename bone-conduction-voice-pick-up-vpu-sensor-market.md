<p><p>Bone Conduction Voice Pickup (VPU) Sensors are classified into two main types: Piezoresistive Pressure Sensors and Piezoelectric Sensors. Piezoresistive sensors detect changes in resistance due to mechanical stress, effectively capturing voice vibrations transmitted through bone. In contrast, Piezoelectric sensors generate an electrical charge in response to applied pressure, offering high sensitivity and accuracy in voice detection. Both types are key in enhancing communication devices, making them more effective in noisy environments by focusing on sound transmitted through bone rather than air.</p></p>
