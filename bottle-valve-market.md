<p><ul><li>Flap Type</li><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Needle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bottle valve market is divided into three main types: flap, diaphragm, and needle. </p><p>Flap type valves use a flexible flap to regulate flow, offering quick open and shut functionality. Diaphragm type valves feature a flexible membrane that allows for precise control of fluid flow, commonly used in applications requiring sterilization. Needle type valves utilize a slender, tapered needle to fine-tune flow rates, making them ideal for applications needing high precision. Each type caters to distinct needs across industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and chemicals.</p></p>
