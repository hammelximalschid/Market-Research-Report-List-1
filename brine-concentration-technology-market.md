<p><p>Brine Concentration Technology (BCT) includes methods like HERO (High-Efficiency Reverse Osmosis), CCD (Closed Circuit Desalination), VTFF (Vacuum Thermal Flash Fractionation), and MVC (Mechanical Vapor Compression). HERO enhances RO efficiency by optimizing pH and reducing scaling. CCD recirculates brine to maximize water recovery. VTFF uses vacuum and heat to evaporate and separate water from brine. MVC compresses vapor to recover water with minimal energy. These technologies address water scarcity by efficiently concentrating brine, reducing waste, and enabling resource recovery in industries like desalination and mining.</p></p>
