<p><p>The Capsule Robot Medical Wireless Endoscopy Market is primarily categorized into two types: Internal Drive Type and Magnetic Drive Type. Internal Drive Type capsules are propelled by tiny motors within the capsule, allowing for active navigation through the gastrointestinal tract. In contrast, Magnetic Drive Type capsules rely on external magnetic fields to maneuver through the body, offering enhanced control and precision. Both types aim to improve diagnostic capabilities and patient comfort during endoscopic procedures by providing non-invasive observation methods.</p></p>
