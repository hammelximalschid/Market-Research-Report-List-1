<p><p>The Central Tire Inflation System (CTIS) market can be divided into two main types: Manual CTIS and Automatic CTIS. Manual CTIS allows drivers to adjust tire pressure manually, offering greater control and flexibility for varied terrain. In contrast, Automatic CTIS automates the pressure adjustment process based on real-time conditions, enhancing convenience and optimizing tire performance without driver intervention. Both types cater to different user needs, with manual systems appealing to those seeking customization and automatic systems favored for ease of use.</p></p>
