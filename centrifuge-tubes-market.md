<p><p>Centrifuge tubes are essential laboratory tools designed for sample separation through centrifugation. The market is segmented into three main types: conical base, round bottom base, and flat bottom base. Conical base tubes allow for efficient sedimentation and easy sample retrieval, making them popular for most applications. Round bottom base tubes are ideal for working with small volumes and are often used in biochemical reactions. Flat bottom base tubes provide stability during handling and are suitable for applications requiring sedimentation in a fixed position.</p></p>
