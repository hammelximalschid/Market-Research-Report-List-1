<p><p>Change and Configuration Management Software (CCMS) is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based CCMS and on-premises CCMS. Cloud-based CCMS is hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, lower upfront costs, and ease of access from anywhere, making it suitable for organizations seeking flexibility. In contrast, on-premises CCMS is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data security and compliance but often requiring higher upfront investment and maintenance costs. Both cater to diverse organizational needs.</p></p>
