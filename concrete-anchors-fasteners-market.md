<p><strong>The Concrete Anchors Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Anchors or Fasteners</li><li>Adhesive Anchors or Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete anchors fasteners market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical anchors and adhesive anchors. Mechanical anchors rely on physical expansion or interlocking mechanisms to secure themselves within the concrete, providing immediate load-bearing capacity. These include wedge anchors and sleeve anchors. In contrast, adhesive anchors utilize a bonding agent, such as epoxy or resin, to adhere to the concrete, offering strong load resistance over time. Each type caters to different applications, load requirements, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
