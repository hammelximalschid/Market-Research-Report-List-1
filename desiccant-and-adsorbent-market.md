<p><p>Desiccants and adsorbents are materials that attract and hold moisture or specific substances from the environment. Desiccants are typically hygroscopic materials that absorb moisture from the air, creating a dry atmosphere. Common examples include silica gel, activated alumina, and certain clays. Adsorbents, on the other hand, are materials that can capture molecules on their surface, a process that can be used for gas separation, purification, and stored materials. Activated carbon is a widely recognized adsorbent.</p><p>The Desiccant and Adsorbent Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing demand across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food packaging, and electronics, where moisture control is crucial.
