<p><p>The die attach machine market encompasses advanced equipment used for attaching semiconductor die to substrates. Two primary machine types are flip chip bonders and die bonders. Flip chip bonders facilitate the direct bonding of chips onto substrates using small solder bumps, allowing for high-density packaging and improved electrical performance. Die bonders, on the other hand, are more versatile, handling various die sizes and materials through different bonding techniques, catering to diverse applications in the semiconductor manufacturing sector.</p></p>
