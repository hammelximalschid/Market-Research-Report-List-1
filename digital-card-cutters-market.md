<p><p>The Digital Card Cutters market encompasses two primary types: Blade Cutting and Laser Cutting. Blade Cutting machines utilize sharp blades to precisely cut materials, ideal for thicker substrates and higher production speeds. In contrast, Laser Cutting employs focused laser beams for intricate designs and detailed cuts, offering superior precision and versatility for various materials. Both methods cater to different industry needs, with Blade Cutting favored for efficiency and volume, while Laser Cutting is preferred for complexity and accuracy in design.</p></p>
