<p><strong>The Digital Crane Scales Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ORS</li><li>OCS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital crane scales are crucial for weighing heavy loads in various industries. The market is primarily divided into two types: Overhead Rail Scales (ORS) and Overhead Crane Scales (OCS). ORS systems are designed for suspended weighing on rail systems, providing precise measurements in manufacturing and shipping facilities. OCS, on the other hand, are integrated into overhead cranes, allowing real-time monitoring of load weights during lifting operations. Both types enhance safety and efficiency in material handling processes.</p></p>
