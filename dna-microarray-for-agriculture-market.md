<p><strong>The DNA Microarray for Agriculture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oligonucleotide DNA Microarrays (oDNA)</li><li>Complementary DNA Microarrays (cDNA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA microarrays are powerful tools in agricultural research, enabling the analysis of gene expression and genetic variations. Oligonucleotide DNA microarrays (oDNA) consist of short, synthetic DNA sequences that can hybridize with target genes, allowing for precise identification and quantification of specific DNA sequences. Complementary DNA microarrays (cDNA), on the other hand, utilize reverse-transcribed cDNA derived from mRNA, facilitating the study of gene expression levels. Together, these technologies enhance crop improvement, pest resistance, and overall agricultural productivity.</p></p>
