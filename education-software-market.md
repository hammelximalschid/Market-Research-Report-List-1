<p><p>The education software market can be categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed and run locally on a school or institution's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing easier access, scalability, and automatic updates. This model reduces upfront costs and reliance on in-house IT, enabling educational institutions to adopt technology more efficiently and flexibly.</p></p>
