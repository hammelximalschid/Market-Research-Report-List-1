<p><p>The electromagnetic crane market comprises three primary types: bridge cranes, portal cranes, and folding arm cranes. Bridge cranes span across a fixed path, ideal for large factories and warehouses, allowing for efficient material handling. Portal cranes operate on a gantry system, commonly used in shipyards and construction sites, offering versatility and mobility. Folding arm cranes, featuring adjustable arms, provide flexibility for various applications, particularly in tight spaces or complex layouts, enhancing operational efficiency in industries requiring precise lifting solutions.</p></p>
