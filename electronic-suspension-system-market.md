<p><p>The Electronic Suspension System market is divided into two main types: Semi-Active Suspension Systems and Active Suspension Systems. Semi-Active systems adjust the shock absorber characteristics in response to road conditions, providing improved ride comfort and handling without significant energy consumption. In contrast, Active Suspension Systems utilize real-time data to actively control the vehicle's suspension components, offering superior performance by minimizing body movements and enhancing stability. Both types aim to improve driving dynamics and passenger comfort in modern vehicles.</p></p>
