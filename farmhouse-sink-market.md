<p><p>The farmhouse sink market primarily features two types: drop-in sinks and under-mount sinks. Drop-in sinks are characterized by their visible rim that rests on the countertop, making installation straightforward and often more accessible for retrofitting existing spaces. In contrast, under-mount sinks are installed beneath the countertop, creating a seamless look and facilitating easy cleaning of countertop debris into the sink. Both types cater to different aesthetic preferences and practical needs in kitchen designs, appealing to a wide range of consumers.</p></p>
