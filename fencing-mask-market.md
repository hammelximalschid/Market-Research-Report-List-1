<p><p>The fencing mask market is categorized into three main types: Foil, Epee, and Sabre. Each type corresponds to a specific fencing weapon and has unique requirements for safety and performance. Foil masks are designed for the lighter, thrusting attacks typical of foil fencing, emphasizing visibility and flexibility. Epee masks cater to the heavier strikes of epee fencing, offering greater durability. Sabre masks feature a design accommodating fast, slashing movements, often incorporating a protective back to secure the wearer during rapid engagements.</p></p>
