<p><p>The fiber cable termination market primarily revolves around two main types: fiber optic pigtails and fanout kits. Fiber optic pigtails are short fiber cables with connectors on one end, designed for easy connection to optical devices, enabling efficient network setups. On the other hand, fanout kits allow multiple fiber strands to be organized and connected from a single unit to various devices, facilitating scalable and organized network configurations. Both products enhance connectivity and streamline installation processes in fiber-optic communications.</p></p>
