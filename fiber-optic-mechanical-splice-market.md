<p><p>The fiber optic mechanical splice market encompasses various coupling types essential for aligning and connecting optical fibers. Flexible couplings allow for some movement, accommodating misalignments. Swivel couplings facilitate rotation, ensuring proper positioning during installation. Capillary couplings feature a small tube to guide fibers precisely, while V-groove couplings offer stable support with a V-shaped channel for alignment. Other couplings may include specialized designs tailored for specific applications or environments, enhancing versatility in fiber optic connectivity solutions.</p></p>
