Basalt Fiber Reinforced Polymer (BFRP) provides excellent thermal resistance, suitable for civil engineering. Aramid Fiber Reinforced Polymer (AFRP) is recognized for its toughness and impact resistance, commonly used in ballistic and protective applications.</p></p>
