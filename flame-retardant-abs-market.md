<p><p>The Flame Retardant ABS market is categorized into Non-halogen and Halogen types based on the additives used. Non-halogen types utilize phosphorus or nitrogen-based compounds, offering eco-friendly and low-toxicity solutions, ideal for applications requiring environmental compliance. Halogen types, containing chlorine or bromine, provide superior flame resistance and are cost-effective, widely used in electronics and automotive industries. Both types enhance fire safety, but non-halogen variants are gaining traction due to stricter environmental regulations and sustainability trends, while halogen types remain popular for their performance and affordability.</p></p>
