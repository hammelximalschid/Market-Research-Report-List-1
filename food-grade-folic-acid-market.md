DHF is an intermediate in folate metabolism, while THF acts as a coenzyme in amino acid and nucleotide synthesis. 5,10-MTHF serves as a key form for cellular processes, playing a crucial role in methylation and DNA synthesis. Each type has distinct applications and benefits in food fortification and dietary supplements, targeting various nutritional needs and health benefits.</p></p>
