<p><p>The food packaging silica gel desiccants market includes three main types: white, blue, and orange desiccants. White silica gel is non-indicating and effectively absorbs moisture without changing color. Blue silica gel contains a moisture indicator, turning pink when saturated, providing a visual cue for effectiveness. Orange silica gel, also an indicating type, changes from orange to green when saturated, offering a similar visual alert. These desiccants are crucial for maintaining food quality by preventing moisture-related spoilage in packaged goods.</p></p>
