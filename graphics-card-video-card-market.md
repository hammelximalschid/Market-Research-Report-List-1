<p><p>The graphics card market is divided into three main types: integrated graphics, discrete graphics, and HD graphics. Integrated graphics come built into the CPU, offering basic performance for everyday tasks and light gaming without extra cost. Discrete graphics cards are standalone units that provide superior performance for demanding applications like gaming and graphic design. HD graphics, a term often associated with enhanced integrated solutions, deliver improved visuals for media consumption and casual gaming, bridging the gap between integrated and discrete options.</p></p>
