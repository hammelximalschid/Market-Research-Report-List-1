<p><p>The gypsum board wall market encompasses various types of boards, primarily gypsum plaster boards and gypsum fiber boards. Gypsum plaster boards, known for their smooth finish, are widely used for interior walls and ceilings, offering excellent fire resistance and sound insulation. In contrast, gypsum fiber boards combine gypsum with cellulose fibers, enhancing durability and moisture resistance, making them suitable for high-humidity environments like bathrooms and kitchens. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, ensuring versatility and efficiency in building projects.</p></p>
