In contrast, the 5-axis machining center can move a tool across five different axes simultaneously, enabling the production of complex geometries and intricate parts with high accuracy. Each type caters to distinct manufacturing needs, driving advancements in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
