<p><ul><li>Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical-Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home wind turbine market primarily features two types: Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT) and Vertical-Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT). HAWTs are characterized by their blades positioned horizontally, effectively capturing wind from various directions, making them suitable for areas with consistent winds. In contrast, VAWTs have a vertical design that allows them to harness wind from any direction, offering advantages in turbulent environments. Both types provide renewable energy solutions for residential use, catering to diverse wind conditions and space requirements.</p></p>
