<p><p>The household solar panel market features two main types: monocrystalline and polycrystalline solar panels. Monocrystalline panels are made from single crystal structures, offering higher efficiency and a sleek design but at a higher cost. In contrast, polycrystalline panels are composed of multiple crystal fragments, making them more affordable but generally less efficient. Both options cater to different consumer preferences based on budget, space, and energy needs, driving growth in the residential solar market. </p></p>
