<p><ul><li>Mechanical Circuit Breaker</li><li>Hybrid Circuit Breaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVDC circuit breaker market comprises two main types: mechanical circuit breakers and hybrid circuit breakers. Mechanical circuit breakers utilize traditional electromechanical mechanisms to interrupt current, offering robust performance for high-voltage applications. In contrast, hybrid circuit breakers combine mechanical components with advanced semiconductor technology, enabling faster and more efficient current interruption. This enhances reliability and reduces system downtime. Both types are essential for ensuring stability and protection in high-voltage direct current transmission systems, given their unique advantages in performance and operational efficiency.</p></p>
