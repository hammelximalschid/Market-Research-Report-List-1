The **EV Drive** market emphasizes electric-only propulsion, using electric motors powered by batteries, often integrated into hybrid systems for zero-emission driving. These technologies collectively aim to improve fuel economy, reduce emissions, and enhance sustainability.</p></p>

<p><p>The hybrid system market includes Mild Hybrid, HEV, PHEV, and EV technologies. Mild Hybrids use a small electric motor to assist the internal combustion engine (ICE), improving fuel efficiency. PHEVs (Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles) offer larger batteries, enabling longer electric-only ranges and can be recharged externally. EVs (Electric Vehicles) run solely on electric power, producing zero emissions, and rely entirely on rechargeable batteries. Each system caters to varying consumer needs for efficiency, range, and sustainability.</p></p>
