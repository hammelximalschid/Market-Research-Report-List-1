<p><p>Hyperspectral imaging cameras are categorized based on their spectral range. Visible/Near Infrared (VIS/NIR) cameras capture light from the visible spectrum to near-infrared, commonly used in agricultural monitoring. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) cameras detect wavelengths for applications like mineral identification. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) cameras are effective in thermal imaging and surveillance. Ultraviolet (UV) cameras analyze materials based on their UV reflectance, useful in forensic investigations. Other market types may include specialized sensors for industrial or scientific applications.</p></p>
