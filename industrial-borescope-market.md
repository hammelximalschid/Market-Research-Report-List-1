<p><p>The industrial borescope market primarily consists of two main types: flexible and rigid borescopes. Flexible borescopes feature a malleable insertion tube that allows for access to complex or confined spaces, making them ideal for inspection in difficult-to-reach areas. Rigid borescopes, on the other hand, have a solid, inflexible design that provides superior image quality and ease of use for straightforward inspections. Both types are crucial for various industries, enabling effective maintenance and quality control by allowing visual access to internal components.</p></p>
