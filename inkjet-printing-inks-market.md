<p><strong>The Inkjet Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drop On Demand (DOD) Inkjet Inks</li><li>Continuous Inkjet Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet printing inks market is primarily categorized into two types: Drop On Demand (DOD) inkjet inks and Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) inks. DOD inks are dispensed in discrete droplets as needed, making them ideal for high-resolution imaging and various applications, including textiles and packaging. In contrast, CIJ inks continuously eject ink, allowing for rapid printing speeds and suitability for high-volume industrial applications, such as coding and marking. Both types cater to specific market needs, enhancing productivity and print quality.</p></p>
