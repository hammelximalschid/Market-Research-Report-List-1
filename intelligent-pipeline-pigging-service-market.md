<p><p>Intelligent Pipeline Pigging Services utilize various technologies to assess pipeline integrity and detect defects. The Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) method detects anomalies by measuring changes in magnetic fields, effectively identifying corrosion and metal loss. Ultrasonic Testing (UT) employs high-frequency sound waves to measure wall thickness and locate flaws, offering precise data on structural integrity. Other methods may include eddy current techniques and thermal imaging, each contributing uniquely to pipeline maintenance and safety by providing comprehensive insights into pipeline condition.</p></p>
