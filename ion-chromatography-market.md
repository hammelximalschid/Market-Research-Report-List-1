<p><strong>The Ion Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Ion Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Ion Pair Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion chromatography market comprises three primary types: Ion Exchange Chromatography, Ion Exclusion Chromatography, and Ion Pair Chromatography. Ion Exchange Chromatography involves the separation of ions based on their charge and affinity for a resin. Ion Exclusion Chromatography separates ions and polar molecules, allowing smaller ions to pass through while excluding larger ones. Ion Pair Chromatography utilizes paired ions to enhance the separation of ionic compounds in non-aqueous solutions, effectively improving the analysis of complex mixtures.</p></p>
