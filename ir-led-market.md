<p><p>The IR LED market can be categorized into two main types: IR LED chips and IR LED packages. These chips are essential components used in various applications, such as remote controls and sensors. These packages are designed for ease of integration into electronic devices, enhancing functionality and reliability in diverse applications.</p></p>
