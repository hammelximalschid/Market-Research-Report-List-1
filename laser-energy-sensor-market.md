<p><strong>The Laser Energy Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyroelectric</li><li>Photodiode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser energy sensor market is primarily segmented into two types: pyroelectric sensors and photodiodes. Pyroelectric sensors detect changes in temperature caused by laser energy absorption, enabling accurate energy measurements. They excel in high-energy pulsed laser applications, providing rapid response times. In contrast, photodiodes operate by converting light into electrical current, suitable for continuous wave lasers. They offer high sensitivity and fast response rates, making them ideal for low-power applications. Together, these technologies serve diverse industrial and research needs.</p></p>
