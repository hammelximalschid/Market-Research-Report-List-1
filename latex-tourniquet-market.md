<p><strong>The Latex Tourniquet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The latex tourniquet market is categorized into two primary types: manual and pneumatic. Manual tourniquets are operated by hand, allowing the user to control the pressure and apply it directly to the limb, making them suitable for a variety of medical procedures. In contrast, pneumatic tourniquets use a mechanical pressure system to deliver consistent and adjustable pressure automatically, enhancing safety and reducing the risk of complications. Both types are essential tools in surgical settings, facilitating hemostasis effectively.</p></p>
