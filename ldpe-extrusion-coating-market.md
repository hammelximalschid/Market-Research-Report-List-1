<p><ul><li>Tubular Process</li><li>Autoclave Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) extrusion coating involves applying a layer of LDPE onto substrates like paper, metal, or plastic for packaging and protection. The Tubular Process involves extruding molten LDPE through a circular die to form a tube that can then be flattened, offering uniform coating. The Autoclave Process, on the other hand, uses a closed chamber to control temperature and pressure for high-quality coatings, ideal for specialized applications. Both methods enhance product durability and barrier properties.</p></p>
