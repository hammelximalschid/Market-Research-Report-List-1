<p><p>The lead-lined metal door market primarily features two types: solid metal doors and hollow metal doors. Solid metal doors comprise a single, thick metal sheet, providing enhanced durability and superior radiation shielding, making them ideal for environments like hospitals and laboratories. In contrast, hollow metal doors are constructed with a frame and insulated core, offering a balance between security and weight. They are often used in commercial applications where radiation protection is necessary but less robust shielding is required.</p></p>
