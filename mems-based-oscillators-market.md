<p><p>The MEMS-based oscillators market features several types, each tailored for specific applications. Voltage Control Oscillators (VCXO) allow frequency adjustment via voltage changes, while Temperature Compensated Oscillators (TCXO) maintain frequency stability across temperature variations. Microcontroller Compensated Crystal Oscillators (MCXO) use a microcontroller for precise frequency control. Spread Spectrum Oscillators (SSXO) reduce electromagnetic interference, Frequency Select Oscillators (FSXO) provide selectable frequencies, and Digitally Controlled Oscillators (DCXO) enable fine frequency adjustments via digital signals. Other oscillators may include hybrid or specialized designs for niche applications.</p></p>
