<p><p>The military drone market comprises three main types: fixed-wing drones, rotary blade drones, and hybrid drones. Fixed-wing drones are designed for long-range missions, offering greater endurance and speed, making them suitable for surveillance and reconnaissance. Rotary blade drones, or quadcopters, provide vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, excelling in maneuverability and close-range operations. Hybrid drones combine features of both types, offering versatility in various missions and environments, catering to diverse military operational needs.</p></p>
