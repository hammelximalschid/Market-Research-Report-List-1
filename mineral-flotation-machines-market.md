<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Machines</li><li>Mechanical Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mineral flotation machines market is primarily divided into two types: pneumatic and mechanical machines. Pneumatic machines use air injection to create bubbles that facilitate the separation of minerals from gangue, often leading to higher recovery rates and efficiency. In contrast, mechanical machines rely on impellers and agitators to achieve flotation, providing better control over the process. Each type serves specific applications in mineral processing, catering to various minerals and operational requirements within the mining industry.</p></p>
