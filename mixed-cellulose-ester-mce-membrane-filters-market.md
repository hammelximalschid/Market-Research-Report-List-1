<p><p>The Mixed Cellulose Ester (MCE) membrane filters market is categorized into two main types: MCE gridded membrane filters and MCE plain membrane filters. MCE gridded membrane filters feature a grid pattern that simplifies colony counting and sample identification, making them ideal for microbial analysis. In contrast, MCE plain membrane filters lack this grid, providing a smooth surface suitable for various applications, including water filtration and laboratory processes. Both types are valued for their excellent filtration efficiency and biocompatibility.</p></p>
