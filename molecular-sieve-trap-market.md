<p><p>The molecular sieve trap market is segmented into two primary types: right angle molecular sieve traps and in-line molecular sieve traps. Right angle molecular sieve traps feature a design that allows for efficient gas flow at a 90-degree angle, making them ideal for compact setups in restricted spaces. In-line molecular sieve traps, on the other hand, are integrated directly into the pipeline, facilitating seamless gas treatment without disrupting the flow. Both types effectively remove moisture and contaminants for various industrial applications.</p></p>
