<p><strong>The MRI Diagnostic Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open MRI</li><li>Closed MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI diagnostic imaging market is primarily divided into two types: open MRI and closed MRI. Open MRI utilizes a wider and more open design, providing a less claustrophobic experience for patients, making it ideal for those with anxiety or larger body types. In contrast, closed MRI features a more traditional, cylindrical design, often providing higher-resolution images and faster scan times. Both types serve distinct patient needs and preferences, influencing market demand and application in clinical settings.</p></p>
