<p><p>The nasal spray bottles market includes two primary types: spray bottles with nebulizers and those with pump systems. Spray bottles with nebulizers are designed to transform liquid medication into a fine mist, allowing for deep inhalation and effective delivery of therapeutic agents, often used in asthma treatments. On the other hand, spray bottles with pump systems utilize a mechanical pump to deliver a consistent, metered dose of medication, favoring ease of use and precise application for nasal congestion and other treatments.</p></p>
