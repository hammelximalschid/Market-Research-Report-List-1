<p><p>The nerve regeneration market consists of several key types: biomaterials, nerve conduits, and nerve protectors. Biomaterials are used to create scaffolds that support nerve healing. Nerve conduits serve as guides for regenerating nerve fibers, facilitating communication between severed ends. Nerve protectors help shield injured nerves from damage during the healing process. Together, these components enhance the body’s natural repair mechanisms, promoting effective recovery from nerve injuries and disorders. Each type plays a vital role in advancing nerve regeneration therapies.</p></p>
