<p><p>The neurofeedback system market comprises several types, each utilizing different techniques to enhance brain function. Slow Cortical Potential Neurofeedback (SCP-NF) targets changes in cortical potential to improve self-regulation. Low-energy Neurofeedback System (LENS) utilizes low-frequency signals to alter brainwave patterns gently. Hemoencephalographic (HEG) neurofeedback measures blood flow changes in the brain, aiding in self-regulation of cognitive functions. Additionally, the "Other" category includes various emerging neurofeedback modalities, offering diverse approaches for therapeutic applications and cognitive enhancement.</p></p>
