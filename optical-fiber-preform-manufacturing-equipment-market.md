<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Preform Manufacturing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Deposition System</li><li>Clad Deposition System</li><li>Core Sintering System</li><li>Clad Sintering System</li><li>Preform Soaking System</li><li>Rod Draw System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber preform manufacturing equipment market includes several key systems. Core deposition systems create the central fiber core by depositing glass layers. Clad deposition systems apply the outer cladding layer, enhancing light guidance. Core sintering systems ensure that the core layers bond effectively through controlled heating. Clad sintering systems perform a similar function for the cladding. Preform soaking systems facilitate the absorption of specific elements for improved fiber properties, while rod draw systems pull the preform into thinner fiber strands for final production.</p></p>
