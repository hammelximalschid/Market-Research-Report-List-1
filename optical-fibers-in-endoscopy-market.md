<p><strong>The Optical Fibers in Endoscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass optical fibers</li><li>Plastic optical fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fibers in endoscopy are crucial for transmitting light and images within medical devices. Glass optical fibers, made from silica, offer high clarity, durability, and resistance to heat, making them ideal for precise medical imaging. Plastic optical fibers, composed of polymers, are more flexible, cost-effective, and lightweight but have lower thermal resistance and image quality compared to glass. Both types cater to different endoscopy needs, with glass fibers preferred for high-performance applications and plastic fibers for disposable or flexible scopes.</p></p>
