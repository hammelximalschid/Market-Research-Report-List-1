<p><strong>The Optical Shaft Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Shaft Encoders</li><li>Absolute Shaft Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical shaft encoders are vital in various industries for precise position and motion feedback. Incremental shaft encoders track relative position changes, generating pulses as the shaft rotates, allowing for real-time speed and direction measurement. In contrast, absolute shaft encoders provide a unique position value for each shaft angle, ensuring accurate tracking even if power is interrupted. Both types cater to diverse applications, with incremental encoders often used in speed monitoring and absolute encoders utilized in applications requiring precise positioning.</p></p>
