<p><strong>The Orthodontic Bracket Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light-Curable</li><li>Chemical-Curable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthodontic bracket adhesives market is characterized by two main types: light-curable and chemical-curable adhesives. Light-curable adhesives are activated by specific wavelengths of light, allowing for quick bonding and easy adjustment during the orthodontic procedure. In contrast, chemical-curable adhesives rely on a chemical reaction for curing, providing a strong bond without the need for light exposure. The choice between these adhesive types often depends on the orthodontist's preference, application specifics, and desired curing speed and strength.</p></p>
