<p><p>The **Paint and Coatings Market** includes various types:  </p><p>- **Solvent-based Coatings**: Use organic solvents for application, offering durability and smooth finishes but with higher VOC emissions.  </p><p>- **Waterborne Coatings**: Utilize water as a solvent, reducing environmental impact and VOC levels while providing good performance.  </p><p>- **High-solids Coatings**: Contain higher solid content, minimizing solvents and emissions, ideal for eco-friendly applications.  </p><p>- **Powder Coatings**: Applied as dry powder and cured under heat, offering durability, no solvents, and minimal waste.  </p><p>- **Others**: Include specialty coatings like UV-cured or anti-corrosive types for niche applications.</p></p>
