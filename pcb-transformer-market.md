<p><strong>The PCB Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Primary, Dual Secondary</li><li>Single Primary, Dual Secondary</li><li>Single Primary, Single Secondary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB transformer market consists of various configurations based on winding setups. Dual primary transformers feature two primary windings, allowing for multiple input voltage options, making them versatile. Dual secondary transformers provide two outputs, suitable for applications requiring different voltage levels. Single primary transformers have one input winding, making them simpler for specific voltage requirements. Lastly, single secondary transformers offer a single output, typically used in straightforward applications. Each type serves distinct applications in voltage regulation, isolation, and power distribution.</p></p>
