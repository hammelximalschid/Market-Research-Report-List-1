<p><ul><li>Water-based Polyurethane Dispersions</li><li>Solvent-based Polyurethane Dispersions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane dispersions market comprises two primary types: water-based and solvent-based dispersions. Water-based polyurethane dispersions are eco-friendly and favored for applications in paints, coatings, and adhesives due to their low volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and ease of use. In contrast, solvent-based polyurethane dispersions offer enhanced performance characteristics and greater durability, making them suitable for industrial applications. Both types are essential in various sectors, including automotive, construction, and textiles, addressing diverse performance needs and environmental regulations.</p></p>
