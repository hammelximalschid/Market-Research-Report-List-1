<p><p>The pool chemical market encompasses various types tailored for water maintenance and sanitation. Trichlor is a slow-dissolving tablet ideal for chlorination. Dichlor, a fast-dissolving granule, is effective for quick chlorine boosts. Cal Hypo provides a strong chlorine solution in granular form. Liquid chlorine offers immediate sanitization, while algaecides combat algae growth. Chemical balancers adjust pH and alkalinity levels for optimal water chemistry. Specialty products serve specific needs, such as protein removal or clarifying water, ensuring safe and clean pool environments.</p></p>
