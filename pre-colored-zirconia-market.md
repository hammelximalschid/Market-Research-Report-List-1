<p><p>The pre-colored zirconia market is primarily segmented into two types: electro-fused zirconium dioxide and chemical zirconium dioxide. Electro-fused zirconium dioxide is produced through the melting of zirconium oxide in an electric arc furnace, resulting in a durable and high-purity material ideal for dental applications and ceramics. On the other hand, chemical zirconium dioxide is synthesized through chemical processes, offering precise control over particle size and composition, making it suitable for various applications, including electronics and coatings.</p></p>
