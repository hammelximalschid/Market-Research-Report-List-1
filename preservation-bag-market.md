PE is favored for its flexibility and moisture resistance, making it suitable for various packaging applications. PVC offers durability and clarity, often used for food and medical packaging. PVDC is known for its superior barrier properties, protecting products from oxygen and moisture, thus extending shelf life. Each type serves distinct needs based on preservation requirements and application settings.</p></p>
