<p><p>The Pressurized Metered Dose Inhaler (pMDI) market features two main types: Manual and Smart pMDIs. Manual pMDIs operate through conventional mechanisms, requiring users to coordinate inhalation with actuation, making them widely used for delivering medications for respiratory conditions. In contrast, Smart pMDIs incorporate digital technology, offering features like dose tracking, adherence reminders, and data connectivity to enhance patient management. This evolution aims to improve treatment adherence and enhance overall patient outcomes in managing respiratory diseases.</p></p>
