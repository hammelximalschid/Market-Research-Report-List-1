<p><p>The residential boiler heating system market primarily encompasses two types: steam boilers and hot water boilers. Steam boilers generate steam for heating by boiling water, providing efficient, high-temperature heat suitable for radiators. Hot water boilers, on the other hand, circulate heated water through a system of pipes and radiators, offering a more comfortable and consistent heat source. Both types cater to residential needs, with varying efficiency, installation costs, and maintenance requirements, influencing consumer preferences and market dynamics.</p></p>
