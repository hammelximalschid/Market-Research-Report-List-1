<p><strong>The Robotic Angiography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CT Angiography Systems</li><li>X-ray Angiography Systems</li><li>MR Angiography Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic angiography system market includes three primary types: CT angiography systems, X-ray angiography systems, and MR angiography systems. CT angiography utilizes computed tomography to produce detailed cross-sectional images of blood vessels, aiding in vascular assessment. X-ray angiography employs traditional X-ray techniques for real-time imaging of blood flow, often used in interventional procedures. MR angiography leverages magnetic resonance imaging to visualize blood vessels without ionizing radiation, providing high soft-tissue contrast, useful in diagnosing vascular conditions.</p></p>
