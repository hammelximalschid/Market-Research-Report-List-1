<p><strong>The Rotational Vestibular Chairs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Wholebody Rotation Chair</li><li>Passive Headon-body Rotation Chair</li><li>Active Headon-body Rotation Chair</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotational vestibular chairs market encompasses three main types. Manual whole-body rotation chairs allow users to be manually turned, providing controlled vestibular stimulation. Passive head-on-body rotation chairs focus on moving the head while the body remains stationary, enhancing the sensory experience without full body rotation. Active head-on-body rotation chairs engage users by incorporating active participation, where the user can control the rotation, offering dynamic vestibular input. Each type serves unique therapeutic and training purposes in vestibular rehabilitation.</p></p>
