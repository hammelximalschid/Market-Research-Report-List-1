<p><p>The single-phase electric power meters market is broadly classified into two types: digital electric power meters and analog electric power meters. Digital electric power meters utilize advanced electronics and microprocessors to provide precise readings, often incorporating features like data logging, remote monitoring, and communication capabilities. In contrast, analog electric power meters rely on mechanical dials and moving parts to indicate energy consumption, offering a simpler, more traditional approach. Both types serve essential roles in measuring electricity usage for residential and small commercial applications.</p></p>
