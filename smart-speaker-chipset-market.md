<p><ul><li>Networked</li><li>Non Networkable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart speaker chipset market can be categorized into two main types: networked and non-networkable. Networked chipsets enable smart speakers to connect to the internet, allowing for streaming services, voice recognition, and integration with smart home devices. These offer enhanced functionality and interactivity. In contrast, non-networkable chipsets are limited to local operations and do not support internet connectivity. They restrict features to basic playback and device control, making them suitable for specific use cases where online access is unnecessary.</p></p>
