<p><p>Social CRM software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based CRM offers flexibility and scalability, enabling businesses to access the software via the internet without heavy infrastructure investments. This model ensures automatic updates and easier collaboration among teams. In contrast, on-premises CRM requires businesses to install and maintain the software on their own servers, providing greater control over data and customization but often involves higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance efforts.</p></p>
