<p><p>The spectroradiometer market is divided into various types, prominently including Atomic Absorption Spectrometers (AAS) and Molecular Absorption Spectrometers. AAS measures the concentration of elements by analyzing the absorption of light from specific atomic transitions, making it ideal for trace metal analysis. In contrast, Molecular Absorption Spectrometers focus on the absorption characteristics of molecules, typically employed in organic and inorganic compound analysis. Both types serve crucial roles in environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and quality control across industries.</p></p>
