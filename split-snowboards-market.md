<p><p>The splitboard market features various camber types to cater to different riding styles and terrain. Normal camber splitboards offer stability and edge control for precise turns on hard-packed snow. Reverse camber boards provide a catch-free ride, ideal for powder and more forgiving landings. Flat camber splitboards bridge the gap, offering stability and versatility. Hybrid types combine features from normal and reverse camber designs, providing a balanced performance for diverse conditions, making them popular among all-mountain riders.</p></p>
