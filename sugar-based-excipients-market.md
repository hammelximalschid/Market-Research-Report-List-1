<p><p>The sugar-based excipients market is categorized into three main types: powder/granule, crystal, and syrup. Powder/granule excipients are widely used for their easy handling and blending properties, often serving as fillers or binders in formulations. Crystal form excipients are valued for their purity and stability, making them suitable for applications requiring precise dosages. Syrups, typically liquid formulations, facilitate easier administration and enhance taste masking in oral medications, providing a comprehensive approach to improving drug delivery and patient compliance.</p></p>
