<p><p>The thermoplastic actuated valves market includes various types designed for fluid control. Globe valves facilitate flow regulation through a spherical body, while ball valves provide quick shut-off with a rotating ball mechanism. Butterfly valves use a rotating disc for flow regulation, and diaphragm valves offer precise control with a flexible membrane. Gate valves provide on/off control with a sliding gate, and other types encompass specialized valves for unique applications. Each valve type serves distinct purposes across industries, emphasizing durability and chemical resistance.</p></p>
