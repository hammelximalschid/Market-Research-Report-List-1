<p><p>Two-way ball valves are classified into three main types based on their material: metal, plastic, and metal with ceramic. Metal ball valves are known for their durability and ability to withstand high pressures and temperatures, making them suitable for industrial applications. Plastic ball valves are lightweight and resistant to corrosion, ideal for chemical handling. Metal with ceramic ball valves combine the robustness of metal with the wear resistance of ceramic, offering high performance in abrasive environments. Each type serves specific operational needs.</p></p>
