<p><p>The Ultra High Purity (UHP) diaphragm valves market includes two main types: pneumatic and manual diaphragm valves. Pneumatic diaphragm valves are operated using compressed air or gas, offering rapid response and automation for precise control in high-purity applications. In contrast, manual diaphragm valves are operated by hand, providing reliable control and simplicity in systems where automation is not critical. Both types are essential in industries like semiconductor manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology, ensuring integrity and minimizing contamination in sensitive processes.</p></p>
