<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Veterinarian X-Ray Flat Panel Detector market comprises two main types: direct conversion and indirect conversion. </p><p>Direct conversion detectors utilize a material that directly converts X-ray photons into electrical signals, producing high-quality images with improved efficiency and reduced radiation exposure. </p><p>In contrast, indirect conversion detectors first convert X-rays into visible light, which is then transformed into electrical signals. This type is generally more widely used due to established technology, offering versatility and cost-effectiveness in various veterinary applications.</p></p>
