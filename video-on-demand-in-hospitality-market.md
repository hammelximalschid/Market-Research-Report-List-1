<p><p>Video on Demand (VOD) in the hospitality market involves delivering on-demand video content to guests in hotels and other accommodations. There are two primary market types: on-premises and cloud. The on-premises model stores content on local servers within the property, offering direct control and potentially lower latency but requiring significant infrastructure investment. In contrast, the cloud market utilizes remote servers, enabling easier scalability, less maintenance, and a broader content library, though it relies on stable internet connections for delivery.</p></p>
