<p><p>WLAN front-end modules encompass several key components essential for wireless communication. Power Amplifiers (PAs) enhance signal strength for transmission, ensuring robust connectivity. Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs) improve the quality of received signals by minimizing noise during the amplification process. Switches facilitate the transition between different paths within the system, optimizing signal flow. Additionally, "Others" may include filters and combiners that refine signal integrity. Together, these elements enhance the performance and reliability of WLAN systems in various applications.</p></p>
