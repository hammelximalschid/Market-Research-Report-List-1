<p><p>The Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) market primarily consists of two main product types: boards and sheets. WPC boards are typically thicker, used for applications like decking, railing, and furniture, offering durability and resistance to weathering. WPC sheets, on the other hand, are thinner and often used for decorative purposes, cladding, and interior applications. Both types combine wood fibers with plastic materials, resulting in eco-friendly products that provide the aesthetic appeal of wood with enhanced longevity and reduced maintenance requirements.</p></p>
